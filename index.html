About

I am currently a PhD student at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. Li Yu. My PhD research is supported by the Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme (HKPFS). Before that, I obtained my Bachelor degree in Engineering also at CUHK, with a major in Artificial Intelligence.

My research interest mainly lies in the interdisciplinary field between AI and healthcare. I am currently working on RNA-related projects that will benefit downstream research in computational biology, meta-genomics and de novo drug discovery.
