About

I am currently a PhD candidate at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. Li Yu. My PhD research is supported by the Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme (HKPFS). Before that, I obtained my Bachelor of Engineering degree (First Class) also at CUHK, with a major in Artificial Intelligence. My current research focuses on developing deep-learning-based models for studying and engineering RNA sequences, structures, and functions.
